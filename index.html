About Me

I am currently a graduate student in IIoT Research Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), China. My advisor is Prof. Xinbing Wang. I am a member of the Intelligent Internet of Things (IIoT) Research Lab at SJTU. I received my bachelor degree from SJTU, and also a Bac+5 degree from Ecole Centrale de Nantes, France.


My research interests include: Systems, Mobile computing, Machine Learning, Crowdsourcing.In SJTU I participated or led several research projects, especially on crowdsourcing (OnTac) and Mobile systems (ARTcode).
